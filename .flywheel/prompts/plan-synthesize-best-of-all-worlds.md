2+ competing plans from different frontier models.

**Goal:** produce a single hybrid plan that integrates the strongest
ideas from every input plan.

**How to use:** open a fresh chat with GPT Pro, paste this prompt with
your original plan, then paste each competing plan one at a time.

to your original plan to turn it into the new, enhanced, much longer
and detailed plan that integrates the best of all the plans with every
good idea included. You don't need to mention which ideas came from
which models in the final revised enhanced plan.
