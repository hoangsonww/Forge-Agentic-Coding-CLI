# Overshoot mismatch hunt ("lie to them")

**Use when:** a review pass feels too short or self-satisfied. Models
tend to stop after finding ~20 issues; claiming many more exist forces
them to keep searching.

**Works for:** plan reviews, bead cross-references, any
comparison/audit task.

---

Do this again, and actually be super super careful: can you please
check over the plan again and compare it to all that feedback I gave
you? I am positive that you missed or screwed up at least 80 elements
of that complex feedback.
