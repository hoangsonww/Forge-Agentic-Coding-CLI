---
name: reality-check
description: Break the spell of local productivity. Ask whether completing all open beads actually closes the gap to the project goal. Use at milestones or when busy agents feel directionally off.
---

Use the verbatim prompt at `.flywheel/prompts/reality-check.md`.

Run at natural milestones (epic closed, ~25% and ~50% of beads done)
and any time the swarm feels busy-but-off.

Actions based on the answer:

- "Yes, all open beads close the gap." → keep going.
- "Close, but X and Y are missing." → revise/add beads. Polish.
- "No, still far." → back to `plan` or `plan-synthesize`.
- "Yes but the plan targets the wrong outcome." → revisit intent.
